Our Story

Welcome To Keelz Treats

Keelz Treats is a reliable food brand in Ibadan, Nigeria. We serve freshly made small chops, grills, and sweet treats with quality and care. From puff-puff, samosas, and spring rolls to tender chicken wings and tasty bites, each order is designed to satisfy. Perfect for homes, offices, and events. We bring flavor and joy right to you.

Known for consistency, creativity, and great service, Keelz Treats delivers more than just food. We create experiences worth sharing. Whether it's a quick snack or a special celebration, every bite is meant to be memorable.
